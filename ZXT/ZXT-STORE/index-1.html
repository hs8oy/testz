About us

We are working diligently to produce high-quality products with beautiful and functional designs suitable for continuous long-term use.

Work has been underway for a long time on developing an expansion plan for ZXT company in Iraq and some other countries like Saudi Arabia is expected that our products will be available for online ordering within this year or the next year.

Our Company

Our company aims to provide a unique and exceptional experience for Iraqi and Arab users in the gaming products sector. We recognize the importance of high temperatures during summer and the significant amount of dust due to the desert nature of our countries. Therefore, we strive diligently to offer products made from high-quality and durable materials that can withstand these harsh conditions. We are committed to excellence and quality to ensure the satisfaction of our customers and meet their needs fully.”

There is a plan to manufacture some products within Iraq, with the possibility of starting the manufacturing of our first product in Iraq in 2025-2026.
